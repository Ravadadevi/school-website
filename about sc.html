    ABOUT US

    Welcome to OPJS-Raigarh

    Founded in 1996 by Syt. Om Prakash Jindal Ji , the O.P. Jindal School, Raigarh (OPJS, Raigarh), patronized by Jindal Steel & Power Ltd, Raigarh, has from its beginning been dedicated to educational transformation in the society. A post-graduate from the University of Texas, Mr. Naveen Jindal, our inspiring Chairman, mentor, multifarious business baron, sportsman and illustrious industrialist, is always keen in improving the educational standards of the society wherever his business is established. The sprawling 31 acres campus, nurtured inch-by-inch and set amidst lush green environment,

    is located close to JSPL, Raigarh. With a hardworking and dedicated team of 345 staff members, the CBSE affiliated school caters to around 5800 students at present, has received the ISO 9001:2015 Certification from IRQS (RvA), proudly received prestigious awards like International School Award (2018-21) from the British Council, National Progressive Schools' Conference (NPSC) membership, All India Rank #1 in NCFE-NFLAT Exam for promoting Financial Literacy among students and Green Campus Award, from e-India. The school lays stress on the all round development of its students in order to meet the challenges of the developing world. The school has also been conferred with UNESCO Accreditation , The Duke of Edinburgh International Award and Certificate of National Operator License to Operate the International Award for Young People (IAYP). Celebration of international days and collaborative projects with various countries to bring internationalism and quality in curriculum, is a regular feature of our system. We are performing leadership roles within the society and nation, in pursuit of the attainment of high ideals. We have associated ourselves with many tasks which aim to achieve the goal of ‘Nation building’ and foster the sense of belongingness and respect for the human values. Our philosophy of education has always been meeting the needs of the individual child

    .

    We believe in imparting quality education to the students so that they may develop intellectual capacity, skills, right attitude in addition to humane and cognitive abilities. The academic and co-curricular programs are based on the School’s mission principles, designed to foster inter-cultural understanding and world citizenship, environmental stewardship, the value of physical labour, and service to humanity with a willing spirit.In the relentless pursuit of these eighteen-years of service, we have established ourselves as one of the leading educational institutions in central India with a mission to facilitate quality education to all sections of the society.
